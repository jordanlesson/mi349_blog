Why Facebook ($FB) is 2021's Stock Pick of the Year
Author: Jordan Lesson Published: 10/14/2021

FB is a great company trading at an undervalued level relative to its future growth opportunity and current dominate market share existence. I believe Facebook is not only in a sector with a wide range of growth opportunity but they are also going to continue dominating the sector as well. Pretty much all of Facebook's business is driven off its users data which is collected interpenetrated and used to sell advertisements to businesses targeting a look alike group. Lately Facebook has come under scrutiny for controversial practices of collecting and selling data on its customers. This led to the justice department talking about how to regulate the social media giant. However what most people don't realize is that not all data is equal. You may not want to share your medical records or tax return statements with Facebook but do you really care if they know your favorite places to eat? A lot of the data Facebook collects is quite harmless yet extremely relevant for companies trying to target ads to their ideal customers. Not only do businesses love when they are seeing high conversion rates from their ads but FB users also get to enjoy having relevant advertisements of products they would like to purchase based on their interdependently chosen feed.

Recently in 2019, technology startups have been spawnings all over the country with some having great success while others suffer and go bankrupt even weeks after opening shop. Unfortunately for tech startups, failure is an option and usually the route that these companies end up going down. There are an infinite ways for a tech startup to fail, however, some companies like eBay, Amazon, and PayPal were able to overcome all the obstacles that come with creating a billion dollar startup because they have excellent management, strict and conservative principles, and a balance of technical and business knowledge.
